Once you have made the initial contact with Dream Build Ltd. And your request has been assessed. A mutual time and date will be arranged to meet at your property to assess with you your required needs. At this point you will be able to discuss in detail your required wishes and it will be addressed if planning will need applying for if not already granted.

Once measurements and listing of your requirement is taken from you and you are happy with what has been discussed, you can expect to wait a short period of time for a written comprehensive quatation to include all labour and materials inclusive within the price.

The next step will be for you to access the quotation carefully and liase if you wish to add/subtract or require some further assistance. Once you are happy with the information provided, Dream Build will contact you for a mutual time slot for a work commencement date. All requirements and what you can expect to take place will be informed to you at this time.

Dream Build does not operated by taking monies up front but works with payment plans that both parties sign to accordance. This gives customers peace of mind and has shown to have been a positive experience with past customers.
